About Us

Your trusted locksmith partner in the DMV area

Your Security Partner in the DMV Area

Advanced Keys & Locks LLC is a professional locksmith company dedicated to providing top-quality security solutions for homes, businesses, and vehicles throughout the Washington D.C., Maryland, and Virginia metropolitan area.

We understand that security is not just about locks and keys - it's about peace of mind. Whether you're locked out of your car at midnight, need to secure your new home, or want to upgrade your business security system, our team is here to help.

Our certified locksmiths bring expertise, professionalism, and a commitment to customer satisfaction to every job. We use state-of-the-art tools and techniques to ensure your security needs are met quickly and effectively.
